# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Harassment, trolling, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
